I've done pioneering work on the automatic application of retention and disposal rules. I wrote [State Records NSW's XML schema for retention and disposal authorities](https://staterecords.nsw.gov.au/recordkeeping/guidance-and-resources/xml-schema-retention-and-disposal-authorities) and created an accompanying desktop drafting application, [Authority Editor](http://srnsw.github.io/).

More recently, as manager of the Digital Archives team at State Records NSW, I supervised [pilot](https://futureproof.records.nsw.gov.au/case-study-internal-pilot-machine-learning-and-records-management/) [projects](https://futureproof.records.nsw.gov.au/case-study-external-pilot-machine-learning-and-records-management/) that used machine learning to automatically apply retention rules to a corpus of official records.
